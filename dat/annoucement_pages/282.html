Binance Lists Wrapped BTC (WBTC)
Binance
2020-08-31 06:11
Fellow Binancians,
Binance will list Wrapped BTC (WBTC) and open trading for WBTC/BTC and WBTC/ETH trading pairs at 2020/08/31 8:00 AM (UTC). Users can now start depositing WBTC in preparation for trading.
Wrapped BTC is a token on Ethereum where the price is pegged to BTC at a rate of 1 WBTC = 1 BTC.
Please ensure that you undertake sufficient risk assessment when trading newly listed tokens, as they are often subject to high price volatility.
WBTC Listing Fee: 0 BNB.
